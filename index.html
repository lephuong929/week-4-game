Pokemon Battle

Mewtwo starts the game with random HP.

There are four pokemon below. By clicking on a pokemon, you will add a specific amount of damage to be dealt.

You win the game by matching your damage dealt to Mewtwo's HP. You lose the game if your damage dealt goes above Mewtwo's HP.

The strength of each pokemon is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the strength of the pokemon.
